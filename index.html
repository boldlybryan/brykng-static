I make digital things

My name is Bryan King and I am a web designer/developer based in Rochester, Michigan. I have the skillset to take a project from ideation to wireframes to polished designs and finally a functioning finished product. I specialize in creating user experiences that are intuitive and accessible. Don't think I'm just an artsy nerd. I have a degree in business, which ultimately helps me deliver you the best possible value on your digital presence.

Here's what I can do for you

Design

All of my work is mobile friendly and responsive design. Whether you need an elegant landing page or have an idea for a new digital product, I'm your guy. Also, I am passionate about creating web style guides to keep your site on brand.

Development

I can take my designs to the next and get them into code. HTML, CSS and JavaScript are my jam, and with them I can create amazing experiences. Whether you need to spin up a new webapp or need help with your e-commerce shop, the buck stops with me.
