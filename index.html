The internet: built by Bryan

Hey there! I'm Bryan King, a web designer and developer living in Rochester, Michigan. I design unique experiences that are enjoyable to use and accessible to a wide audience. I'm able to do this with my eye for beautiful design and a focus on utilizing the best technology for the job. I take pride in delivering a site that allows your business to thrive online. Taking your project from start to finish

Design

All my work is mobile-first, meaning I focus on optimizing a site for a smartphone and work my way to the big screens. Having a responsive website is not only important for usability, but also factors into your Google search ranking. Whether you're in need of a new landing page or a complete redesign of your website, I'm here to build those for you.

Development

Once I have a design, I take them to the next step and get them into code. HTML, CSS and JavaScript are my specialities that allow me to craft a one-of-a-kind home on the web. Whether you need a new web application or need help with your ecommerce shop, you can count on me to deliver.

Loan Officer Profiles

Flagstar Bank has over 200 loan officers that work hard to get customers into the home of their dreams. My job was to create of a template for the profiles and a invent a CSS library for our responsive site. I'm glad to say that this project was a success! Location Pages

With over 100 locations around the country, Flagstar needed to provide a way for customers to learn more about their neighborhood branch. As a part of a renovation of our Home Loans section, I was tasked with redoing the location pages. Visitors can see contact information, services offered, and the loan officers assigned to a branch. My work included restructuring the markup of the existing pages, utilizing Bootstrap to accomplish the layout. Also I wrote new CSS to comply with a new style.

KingCSS Framework

While I designed and developed my portfolio, I took it as an opportunity to build a new CSS library for my own personal use. Based off the principles in atomic design and functional CSS, I made a system that made sense to me. I'm pleased with its small size, which allows me to create sites that put performance first. Also, it allows for semantic markup, which translates into a site that is both a11y and SEO friendly. It is constantly a work in progress and I look forward to using it on future projects.
